SYMPTOMS OF COVID 19

It may take about 7-14 days to show the symptoms of coronavirus. Coronavirus shows human to human transmission, making it more spreadable. The prominent symptoms of covid 19 are as follows-

  1. Fever - Body temperature above 980F might be a symptom.
  2. Sore throat
  3. Shortness of breath
  4. Tiredness

Some less common symptoms-

  • aches and pains
  • diarrhoea
  • loss of taste or smell
  • a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes
  • conjunctivitis

Given below are some of the serious symptoms of coronavirus in a human body. If they are shown in a body, the person must concern a doctor for checkup immediately.

  1. difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  2. chest pain or pressure
  3. loss of speech or movement
